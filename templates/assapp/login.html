Processed Assent Data

This website is designed to manage processed assent data efficiently. When a request is raised in Greencheck, it is logged here along with detailed information, including customer details and parts data (total parts, matched parts, and unmatched parts). Only matched parts are processed in Greencheck, but both matched and unmatched parts are logged here for better clarity and easy reference.
